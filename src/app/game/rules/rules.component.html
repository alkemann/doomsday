Rules of Doomsday

Score 1 point for each correctly "guessed" weekday. Lose 1 point for each mistake.

Doomsday calulcations are done by knowing a year's "doomsday" and then calculating from the nearest one to the target date.

Years

A year's weekday is calculated from the century's by adding 1 per year, plus 1 extra per leap year.

2000s are Tuesday (2). 2004 would then be 4 + 1 days later, Saturday. For 1900s it was Wednesday (3)

The century also "reset" on 28, 56 and 84. Meaning 2028, 2056 and 2084 also have Tuesday as Doomsday.
